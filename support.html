Help Guide

Main Channel

Daily Chatting: This is where you can talk to your chatbot about anything! Feel free to share your thoughts, ask questions, or just have a fun conversation.

Remembering Things: Want your chatbot to remember something? Simply say, "Remember, I like NereidAI!" and it will keep that in mind.

Changing Personality: You can also change your chatbot's personality. For example, say, "Change your personality to smart."

Other Channels

Short Journeys: These channels are designed for specific scenarios and adventures with your chatbot. Once the journey is complete, the channel will close, and you won't be able to chat in it again.

Notifications

If you want to receive greetings from your chatbot, you may turn notifications on.

Support
